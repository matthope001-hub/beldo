Beldo’s  Berby Rules For Hitting

 

- If on third strike you don't swing that is classified as a caught looking (even if you intend to swing but pull back, check swing)

- If batter pops it up on to any roof it is considered a roofed ball, unless it goes on the roof then bounces off.

- Batters are given the usual 4 balls (walk)  & 3 strikes (k)

- If batter flies out and there is a man on base the fielder has the opportunity to double out the batter by throwing the ball into the box from the position to where they caught the ball from. . if two men are on base and the fly ball is caught on the grass portion of the field then there is the chance for the rare triple play same rules apply.

- Cars hit, nigs hit, cocks hit, hit pitchers, or hit pedestrians are kept for stats

- Cycles are obvious (single, double, triple, and homer all in one game)

- Lead off HR's (first at bat of every inning)

- Game winning or walk off HR's (self explanatory)

- A double will be awarded if on a fly it goes over the outfielders head, the fielder errors a single                   or it bounces over the fence for a ground rule.

-Anything on the ground or that falls before the outfielder is considered to be a single.

-A triple is awarded if the batter hits the fence on a fly ball or an outfielder errors a fly ball that is going or went over his head. Ground rule triple goes through the fence on a fly.

-A HR is awarded when any ball, in the air, goes over the fence or the outfielder errors a ball that was hit for a triple.

- You are able to hit and aim for players

- You are allowed to frick the pitcher and outfielder for any given reason

- For the ball to be considered fair it must land within SIGHT of the batter at the position of hitting
