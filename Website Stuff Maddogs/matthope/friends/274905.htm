dinner time @ 582 upper wellington
so lyle and i's apartment is not only two houses down the street from a 7-11, but also directly across the street from a funeral home, a radio station, and an irish grill called oniell's.

we tried it out one of the first nights we were here, and were shocked to learn they had something called the 'pugger burger'.

which is a pound ((yes. a POUND. AS IN ONE.)) beef patty, charbroiled, and then dressed with cheese, condiments, and bacon.

bacon. on a ONE POUND burger. seems like overkill. oh and it comes with a side of onion rings.




it's rather terrifying if you look directly into it.


i, being on the more sensible side ((and loving garlic and onions)), opt for the 'lose a friend burger', which is 1/4 patty with garlic mayo, pickles, and onions. yummm.

ps - their food is very, very good. AND CHEAP.
