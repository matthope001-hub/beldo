Individual Statistics

Individual player stats are tracked for every game played. The following categories are recorded:

Stat Description Formula
Games (G) Total games played —
At Bats (AB) Total plate appearances —
Runs (R) Total runs scored —
Hits (H) Total base hits —
Doubles (2B) Two-base hits —
Triples (3B) Three-base hits —
Home Runs (HR) Four-base hits —
Runs Batted In (RBI) Runs driven in —
Batting Average (AVG) Hits per at bat H ÷ AB
Slugging % (SLG) Total bases per at bat TB ÷ AB
Errors (E) Defensive errors —
