Nicole & Matt's NHL Stats

Hockey statistics and tracking

About This Section

This section is dedicated to tracking NHL statistics, following favorite teams, and monitoring player performances throughout the season. From the early 2000s to present day, hockey stats have been a passion project.

Team Tracking

Over the years, various teams have been followed and analyzed. The stats include:

  • Team standings and records
  • Player statistics
  • Goalie performance metrics
  • Special teams (power play, penalty kill)
  • Season trends and analysis

Historical Data

The NHL stats collection spans multiple seasons, with particular focus on:

2000-2001 Season

A memorable season with intense playoff races and standout individual performances.

2001-2002 Season

Another exciting year of hockey with emerging stars and veteran leadership.

Olympic Years

Special attention given to Olympic years when NHL players participated, showcasing the best talent on the international stage.

Stats Categories

Category Description
Goals (G) Total goals scored
Assists (A) Total assists
Points (P) Goals + Assists
Plus/Minus (+/-) Goal differential when player is on ice
Penalty Minutes (PIM) Total time in penalty box
Power Play Goals Goals scored on power play
Shorthanded Goals Goals scored while shorthanded
Game Winning Goals Goals that put team ahead to stay
Shots (SOG) Total shots on goal
Shooting % Goals / Shots percentage

Goalie Stats

  • Wins (W) and Losses (L)
  • Ties (T) / Overtime Losses (OTL)
  • Goals Against Average (GAA)
  • Save Percentage (SV%)
  • Shutouts (SO)
  • Minutes Played
